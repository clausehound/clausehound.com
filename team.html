About Us

Cobalt Lawyers was founded by business lawyers who are committed to the success of their clients. The Cobalt legal team is an affiliation of accomplished corporate finance and securities law professionals with substantial industry experience who advise leading Canadian and international clients on their transactional activities. Cobalt Lawyers and the independent consultants who provide counsel to Cobalt or its clients have in-depth corporate finance, M&A and technology law experience.
