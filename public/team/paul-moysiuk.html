Paul Moysiuk

Of Counsel

Paul Moysiuk is a Shanghai-based Canadian lawyer who specializes in providing services to assist international clients with establishing, operating and developing businesses in China. His main clients include Canadian businesses and a range of sports and entertainment companies, from start-ups to world famous, entering into or operating in China’s business markets.

His main practice areas include the establishment of legal structures for his clients through holding companies in Hong Kong, Singapore, Guernsey or Caymans, contract drafting, legal research and applications for licenses and permits (e.g. performance licenses, ticket sales licenses, permits for athletes and entertainers, etc.), providing intellectual property theft protection mechanisms and registrations and dispute resolution services regarding intellectual property theft and international trade.

Paul obtained his Juris Doctorate law degree at Western University in London, Ontario and was called to the Canadian Bar in Alberta. At the beginning of his career Paul developed his skills working under the mentorship of Richard McLaren, a world-renowned lawyer, academic and sports law specialist. Afterwards and prior to establishing his own practice, Paul joined a leading Canadian business law firm Blakes, Cassels & Graydon LLP in Calgary, Alberta before reallocating to Shanghai, China to work for a top-ranked Zhong Lun Law Firm. With his knowledge and understanding of Western and Asian legal and business systems, Paul plays a valuable role for the international businesses he assists in China and Asia.
