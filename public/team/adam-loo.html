Adam Loo

Of Counsel

Dual J.D, uWindsor/Detroit Mercy

B.A (Hons.) Legal Studies, uWaterloo

As a long time entrepreneur, Adam has been on all fronts of small business, from creative technology ideation to business development.

Adam is also a tech founder, where he experienced first hand the challenges with growing his own business, which motivated him to move on to his new endeavour - to support the growth of small businesses and startups as a lawyer. The goal - to make the legal processes for entrepreneurs painless, efficient and effective. He joined Cobalt Lawyers based on the shared vision.

Adam attained his Dual JD degree from the University of Windsor and the University of Detroit Mercy, which created a unique experience to be exposed to cross-border/international commercial relationships in a legal context. His special interest in the law and entrepreneurship led him to become a key member of the Law, Technology and Entrepreneurship Clinic at the University of Windsor. As an undergraduate student at the University of Waterloo, he worked several years as a Digital Media Producer, exercising his passion for digital media technology.

Outside of servicing the entrepreneurial community, you can find Adam engineering records in the sound recording studio or taking on various projects to develop mini-apps and design interactive websites.
