Pricing

FREE SCOPING CALL. Book a call with the Cobalt legal team to determine your needs for a potential engagement.

STRATEGIC REVIEW: This is a thirty minute call that includes pre-processing of document(s) using Clausehound's unscrambling technology (described below), to identify document "gaps", and to provide access to the 2,500 question and answer database). During this call, missing items are flagged, discussion of risks on the approach taken versus desired outcome, and recommendations on how to improve the document.

DEEP AND DETAILED REVIEW: This is a (+/-) thirty minute call that includes pre-processing of document(s) to identify gaps, as described under strategic review. Our lawyers will also take a deeper dive, and will proofread requested legal language to look for drafting errors, confusing language, and to address other risk points.

INCORPORATION AND OTHER KEY SMALL BUSINESS LEGAL DOCUMENTS. For more details on incorporation, or questions on the cost and scoping of other documents, inquire on scoping call.
