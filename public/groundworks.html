Cobalt Lawyers acquires Groundworks, offers tech-assisted commercial real estate services

Toronto, ON - January 31, 2022

Cobalt Business Counsel Professional Corporation (Cobalt Lawyers) announced today their acquisition of Groundworks Commercial Leasing (Groundworks), an Ontario-based law firm that offers commercial real estate leasing services through a team of vetted Canadian legal and lease professionals.

This acquisition combines Cobalt's long standing presence of providing tech-assisted commercial legal services to Ontario SMEs, with Groundworks' commitment to providing high quality work with a fast turnaround time and within budget. Groundworks' services include, but are not limited to, standard lease abstraction services, lease analysis, lease creation and tenant estoppel certificate services. These services now join Cobalt's areas of expertise, in a working environment that has the same SME-minded ethos that both Groundworks and Cobalt was founded on.

"From early on, our clients expressed their need for more efficient and cost-effective services, and we built deal automation technology to fill that need," says Rajah Lehal, CEO and Founder of Cobalt Lawyers. "Without the technology, we wouldn't be able to provide services to the same caliber that we do."

Groundworks has been servicing clients in Ontario for more than 6 years, with a commitment to providing value-add work in the most efficient means possible. In prioritizing efficiency, Groundworks has been seen as a pioneer in its industry with differentiators such as being a completely virtual firm, and always striving to make the most of their clients' time. With the acquisition, Groundworks' founder and lawyer, Natalka Falcomer, will be joining Cobalt's team to consult and assist in a smooth transition.

Natalka is a lawyer who is deeply committed to increasing access to justice and positively changing the way that the law is practiced and delivered. Her passion to help make legal advice affordable and practical, as well as her desire to give lawyers the opportunity to work in a friendly and flexible atmosphere, led her to found Groundworks Realty Services. Natalka sits on various real estate boards and property technology incubators, and has recently joined the Ontario Trillium Foundation as Chair of the Granting Committee. For all of these efforts, she's been recognized as Real Estate Professional's Elite 100 and is a sought after panelist and guest lecturer at various law schools and forums.

Cobalt's acquisition of Groundworks will allow Cobalt to expand into the commercial real estate industry and to integrate Cobalt's proprietary deal automation technologies into leasing services. "This is a natural partnership given the quality of service Cobalt Lawyers provides, using innovative processes," said Natalka Falcomer, Founder of Groundworks. "Cobalt has a strong history of providing legal support to SMEs in ways that make sense for their business - creating a one stop shop for anyone looking to set up their small business."

About Cobalt Lawyers

Cobalt Lawyers is a boutique business law firm founded by business lawyers who are committed to the success of their clients. Cobalt's lawyers have in-depth corporate finance, real estate, corporate structuring, M&A, privacy, and technology law experience and have helped thousands of individuals, new ventures, scaling startups and enterprises with legal services since 2009.
