Fast Review of Large Contracts

We understand that reviewing an endless amount of non-disclosure agreements (NDA’s) and master services/software services agreements (MSA’s) can be tedious and time-consuming.

Cobalt Lawyers licenses Clausehound’s document comparison software in order to efficiently review various agreements, including NDA’s and MSA’s. With the use of Clausehound, Cobalt is able to offer friendly pricing options including success-fee options to businesses with a high frequency of large-size (in terms of number of pages to review) contracts.

Instead of spending time reading, comprehending and revising complex language in such agreements, our clients have used that time for their business and product development initiatives.

Outsourced In-House Counsel

Cobalt’s pricing is offered to growth-stage clients, who we provide legal support to for day-to-day activities, at a discounted rate. Any unused hours are always carried forward to future months until the end of the calendar year.

Our lawyers will provide a quality-control lens on legal documentation sent out or received by our clients, to ensure that they are operating on a sound legal foundation. Ongoing legal attention of this nature would normally be difficult for a growth company without hiring dedicated in-house counsel.
